Meet Your Teacher

Serghei is an accomplished teacher from Ukraine, where he has coached students and acted as a judge for prestigious ballroom competitions.

With over 15 years of experience, he is known for his expertise in Latin and ballroom dance, as well as his passion for helping students find confidence through dance. His dedication to the art form has earned him accolades across Europe, and he continues to inspire dancers of all ages and levels.
